# Electricity
## Current and Charge
### Current
#### Rate of flow of charge (rate of flow of electrons)
### Charge Flow
#### The number of electrons
### Equation
#### Q = I * t
#### charge (C) = current (A) * time (s)
#### current (A) = charge (C) / time (s)
